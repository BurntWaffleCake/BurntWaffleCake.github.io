Goose Game

Goose Simulator: A Not So Ordinary Goose Game

Goose simulator takes inspiration from the wacky nature of games such as Deer Simulator and goat Simulator in order to provide the player with a with a comical yet intuitive experience. The game will run on the Roblox engine due to the multiplayer compatibility the engine provides along with the free price tag and monetization opportunities. The game will also take inspiration from games such as Valorant and Overwatch with PVP mechanics such as skills and a team VS team structure. We plan to expand our game to mobile and other devices supported by Roblox if our game is successful in gaining traction and eventually to Steam and other game engines that are more powerful than Roblox's.
