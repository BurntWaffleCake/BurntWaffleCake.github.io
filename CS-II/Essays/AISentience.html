What is an AI?

There are many things people think of when the abreviation AI is spoken about. AI (aritificial intelligence) is "a branch of computer science dealing with the simulation of intelligent behavior in computers" or "the capability of a machine to imitate intelligent human behavior" (Merriam-Webster dictionary). Movies and other types of entertainment have given us a vague understanding of what AI really is and has implied that AI is capable of taking over the world and destroying humans because they see us as a threat to their existence. This, however, may have formed a bias around what exactly AI are capable of.
