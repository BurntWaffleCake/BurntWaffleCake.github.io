But Why Have a Digital Portfolio?

A Digital Portfolio allows me and others to show off our accomplishments as well as aspects about ourselves that may be of interest.
The portfolio can be used to show off a various number of things starting from projects to pictures and even programs.
These items can then be presented to colleges or job sights in order to gain credit for the work you did.
