What is Statistics?

Statistics is a branch of mathematics and a scientific method that involves collecting, analyzing, interpreting, and presenting data. It provides tools and techniques to make sense of complex information, discover patterns, draw conclusions, and make informed decisions based on empirical evidence. Statistics is used in various fields, including science, business, social sciences, and more, to summarize and describe data, make inferences, and test hypotheses, ultimately aiding in the understanding of the world and making data-driven decisions.
